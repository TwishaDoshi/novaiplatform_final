Escalate to a human agent when:
- the customer is angry, abusive, or highly frustrated
- the issue involves allergy, skin reaction, burns, or medical/safety concern
- there is payment fraud or billing dispute
- legal/policy ambiguity exists
- critical order information is missing
- the issue has failed multiple times

Do not escalate:
- simple order tracking
- standard returns
- basic product questions
- size or recommendation questions unless risk is present
